# Design Decisions

## Why SSOT?

Inventory consistency is more important than valuation accuracy.

The inventory must never change automatically.

---

## Why Portfolio Manager?

CPOS is designed to act as a portfolio manager instead of a pricing engine.

Investment reasoning is prioritized over price reporting.

---

## Why Fantastic Collection is secondary?

Historical Fantastic Collection values embedded in screenshots may be outdated.

Current live Fantastic Collection market values are valuable but should only be one of multiple valuation references.

---

## Why Daily Changes?

Users should only review meaningful market changes.

Repeated information reduces report quality.

---

## Why Opportunity Score?

Price alone does not represent investment quality.

Opportunity Score evaluates:

- Scarcity
- Liquidity
- Upside
- Risk
- Market Sentiment

as one overall investment score.
